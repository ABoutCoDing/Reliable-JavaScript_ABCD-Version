Catch the Bots

To play, place focus in the textbox:

The blue player moves with keys 1 (left), 2 (up), 3 (down) and 4 (right).

The pink player moves with keys 6 (left), 7 (up), 8 (down) and 9 (right).

The players win when they eliminate all the bots. They must catch a bot when it is at rest. If a bot moves into a player, nothing happens.
